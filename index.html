About

American Jewish World Service (AJWS) works to realize human rights and end poverty in the developing world.


What They Needed

AJWS has a small but mighty digital team and were looking for someone that could help them with analytics support, SEO strategy and campaign strategy.


What I did


Google Analytics and Tag Manager

I helped AJWS improve their data collection methods by implementing additional tracking in Google Analytics and GTM, and setting up dashboards that helped AJWS better understand the different types of traffic on their site and how that was contributing to site goals. While working with AJWS, I analyzed a number of campaigns, providing concrete understand of the campaigns contribution to their organization goals and suggestions for increased impact on future campaigns.


SEO

After doing a thorough SEO audit of AJWS.org, I worked with AJWS' technical team to implement technical improvements for the site and provide training for the editorial staff on content-based strategies for increasing SERP rankings. After implementing a number of improvements across key pages, we saw a 148% improvement on impressions and a 150% improvement on clicks month over month.


Campaign Development

For the Jewish New Year, I advised on the strategy behind AJWS' #18Words campaign and created the mock and UX design for the campaign's landing page. After the campaign wrapped, I did a full analysis of the campaign data for the team, providing suggested improvements for future campaigns.


Content Strategy

AJWS has a lot of great content on the site, but they felt it needed consolidation. I worked with the digital team create a plan and mocks for consolidating pages and to further focus their navigation to make their site easier and more purposeful for the user.

  • Client: American Jewish World Service
  • Service: Strategy

About

Choonimals is a clothing company based in Brooklyn, NY.


What They Needed

Choonimals was looking for someone that could help them with their site migration, data collection and analysis, and advertising strategy as they looked to increase their online presence.


What I did


Google Analytics and Tag Manager

I set up Choonimals' data collection and analysis system, building custom dashboards, integrating e-commerce tracking and helping them execute on custom tracking for all marketing and email campaigns. Because of this, Choonimals was able to determine the marketing campaigns that were working best, shifting their strategy to focus on the campaigns that resulted in sales.


Digital Advertising

I implemented pixel and UTM advertising tracking for Choonimals, allowing them to track all advertising conversion from platform to site. We implemented an evergreen retargeting campaign through Facebook that allowed Choonimals to convert cart abandonment to conversion and create a positive return on ad dollars spent.


Web Development

I helped Choonimals launch their new online store and migrate to a new platform that would increase their ability to serve and manage their customer base.

  • Client: Choonimals Clothing Company
  • Service: Strategy, Marketing and Web Development
